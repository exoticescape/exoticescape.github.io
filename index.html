How do we make it?

Our Exotic Escape is a delicious tropical blend, made from the finest handpicked, locally sourced, genuine ingredients. We use meticulously juiced oranges, crushed and blended coconut, mashed banana, juicy mango, delicious passionfruit and crunchy pineapple. This incredible fusion creates the perfect start to your day, giving you a zing of much needed freshness.
